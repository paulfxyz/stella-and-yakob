# Stella & Yakob

> *Un livre illustré pour enfants sur l'IA, la curiosité et la sagesse*
> *A illustrated children's book about AI, curiosity, and wisdom*

---

## About the Book

**Stella** is a small girl with a dark brown bob and a pale blue dress. One grey afternoon, she hears grown-ups talking about something called "artificial intelligence" and feels a knot in her stomach. She doesn't understand — and that's scary.

Then **Yakob** appears: a large, wise owl with warm brown feathers and big amber eyes. Together they go on an adventure through time and imagination — discovering that AI is a tool made by humans, powerful but not human itself.

But there's a twist: the **Silver Fox** (pages 17–22) — a sleek, silver-furred trickster who promises shortcuts and dazzling answers. Will Stella be fooled by glittering shortcuts, or trust her own curiosity, kindness, courage, and creativity?

A story about wonder, discernment, and why humans will always bring what machines cannot.

## Characters

**Stella** — Small girl, short dark brown bob hair, simple pale blue or grey dress, rosy cheeks, big expressive eyes. Always consistent across all illustrations.

**Yakob** — Large natural owl, warm brown feathers with amber/gold tones, big round amber eyes full of warmth and wisdom. Natural — no glasses, no accessories.

**Le Renard Argenté** *(pages 17–22)* — Sleek fox, metallic silver fur, sharp glittering eyes, wide dazzling smile, cold electric shimmer.

---

## Illustration Style

> *Minimal loose watercolor. Very sparse, hand-painted. Lots of white paper showing. Soft loose brushstrokes, barely sketched.*

Warm cream backgrounds, soft amber tones, tiny golden sparkles. 4:3 raw illustrations composed into square pages with a clean text zone below.

## Story Arc

| 4–5 | Yakob appears — introduces himself with golden sparks |
